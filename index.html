Challenge

The Problem & Our Solution

Existing tissue recovery methods are often invasive, expensive, or inaccessible to everyday patients.

The Problem

Conventional wound healing and pain management approaches fall short in several critical areas:

  • Prolonged recovery times in musculoskeletal injuries
  • High cost of clinical therapy equipment
  • Limited portability — patients must visit clinics
  • Pharmacological side effects from pain medication
  • No non-invasive, dual-modality home-use solution

Our Solution

ElectraHeal combines two clinically studied modalities into one compact, wearable device:

  • 660 nm red light stimulates cellular ATP production
  • Microcurrent (below 1mA) accelerates ion transport
  • Battery-powered — fully portable, clinic-free
  • Non-invasive and drug-free therapy approach
  • Prototype validated via agar gel diffusion experiments
About the Device

What is ElectraHeal?

ElectraHeal is a non-invasive, dual-modality wearable prototype designed to accelerate soft tissue recovery and reduce localized pain. It integrates red light photobiomodulation with sub-sensory microcurrent stimulation into a single compact form factor, empowering patients to undergo evidence-based therapy at home.

Key Features

Every component is purposefully designed for effective, safe, and accessible therapy.

Microcurrent Stimulation

Delivers sub-threshold electrical current (< 1mA) that mirrors the body's own bioelectric signals, promoting accelerated ion transport and cellular repair.

Red Light Therapy

660 nm wavelength penetrates superficial tissue layers, stimulating mitochondrial activity and boosting ATP production for enhanced cellular regeneration.

Portable Design

Compact, wearable form factor enables untethered use at home or on the go — eliminating the need for clinic-based therapy equipment.

Low Power Operation

Engineered for energy efficiency with a 6V battery supply, minimizing power consumption without compromising therapeutic output.

Non-Invasive & Safe

All outputs are within physiologically safe ranges. No needles, no drugs, no thermal damage — safe for daily use with no reported adverse effects.

Adjustable Control

Integrated potentiometer allows precise current regulation, enabling customization of therapy intensity for different patient needs and tissue states.

Mechanism

How It Works

Two scientifically grounded modalities work in synergy to promote tissue recovery.

Red Light Photobiomodulation

660 nm wavelength · Mitochondrial activation

Red light at 660 nm is absorbed by cytochrome c oxidase in the mitochondrial respiratory chain. This triggers a cascade of photochemical events, increasing electron transport efficiency and dramatically boosting ATP synthesis.

  • 1
    660 nm photons penetrate skin and superficial tissue
  • 2
    Absorbed by cytochrome c oxidase in mitochondria
  • 3
    Electron transport chain activity increases
  • 4
    ATP synthesis elevated → cellular energy available
  • 5
    Accelerated tissue repair and reduced inflammation

Microcurrent Stimulation

< 1 mA · Bioelectric signal mirroring

Microcurrent therapy applies electrical current that closely mirrors the body's own bioelectric signals. At sub-sensory levels, this current enhances ion transport across cell membranes, accelerating the natural healing cascade.

  • 1
    Current generated at sub-threshold levels (< 1mA)
  • 2
    Applied via surface electrodes on target tissue
  • 3
    Enhances Na⁺/K⁺ ion transport across membranes
  • 4
    Protein synthesis and fibroblast activity increase
  • 5
    Accelerated collagen formation and tissue rebuilding

Safety

Safety by Design

Every output level is carefully bounded within established safe physiological thresholds.

Safe Current Levels

Microcurrent output is maintained strictly below 1mA — far beneath the threshold for sensory perception or tissue damage.

Eye Safety

The 660 nm LED array operates at low intensity. Avoid direct eye contact; protective eyewear recommended during use near the face.

Drug-Free Therapy

Zero pharmaceutical components — completely non-pharmacological. Eliminates risks associated with drug interactions or side effects.

Non-Invasive

No skin penetration. Surface-level electrodes and LED contact ensure treatment remains entirely external and non-invasive.

No Thermal Risk

Both LED output and microcurrent levels generate negligible heat. No risk of thermal tissue damage during normal operation.

Contraindications

Not recommended for use over implanted electronic devices, during pregnancy, or on open wounds without professional supervision.
